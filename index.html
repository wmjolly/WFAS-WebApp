About WFAS

WFAS is an integrated, web-based platform providing real-time and forecasted national views of fire weather and fire potential across the United States. Access critical data, including:

  • National Fire Danger Ratings (NFDRS)
  • Moisture Estimates (1-hr to 1000-hr)
  • Drought Indices (Keetch-Byram, Palmer)
  • Satellite-Derived Vegetation Conditions

Whether you are a wildland firefighter, local, regional or national fire manager, industry risk manager, or concerned citizen, WFAS provides the multi-temporal and multi-spatial insights needed to support informed fire management decisions and reduce potential wildfire risk.
